The Best Books According to Shraddha

My top 3 books of all time

1. Yayati

This a fictional book .
It talks about how everyone is right in there own place in life and how should we mold our perspective while treating people.

2. Prakashwata

This is a book by Dr. Prakash Amate.
He is a social worker who works for the tribal community in Maharashtra.
He talks about his journey into the social work and what inspires him to do the same

3. Swami Vivekanand

This book is written by the disciples of the Swami Vivekanad.
The book talks about Swamiji's thoughts on the various topics such as education, sprituality and patriotism .
